Our Methodology

How we test and rank AI tools for solopreneurs.

Our Testing Process

We spend a minimum of 20 hours with each tool. We test them on real solopreneur tasks like building features, automating workflows, content creation, and research.

Scoring Criteria

  • Productivity Impact (30%)
  • Ease of Use (25%)
  • Value for Money (20%)
  • Reliability & Accuracy (15%)
  • Future Potential (10%)
