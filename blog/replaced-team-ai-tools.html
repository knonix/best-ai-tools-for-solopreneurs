I Replaced My $8k/month Team With These 5 AI Tools

For 18 months I was paying $8,000 every month for a virtual assistant, developer, designer, and content writer. Then I discovered I could replace almost all of them with AI.

The 5-Tool System That Runs My Business

  1. Cursor — My full-time developer. I describe features in normal English and it builds, debugs, and ships production code.
  2. Claude 4 — My strategist, writer, and project manager. It creates content calendars, writes emails, and even manages my task list.
  3. Perplexity Pro — My 24/7 research team. No more tab hell or hours wasted on Google.
  4. Zapier + Make.com — My operations and customer support department. They run on autopilot.
  5. v0 + Framer + Gamma — My entire design department.

The Results After 90 Days

  • Monthly expenses down by ~$7,200
  • Output increased by 4x
  • More creative control and faster iteration
  • Actually enjoying the work again
