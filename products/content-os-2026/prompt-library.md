# Content OS 2026 - Prompt Library

## Core System Prompts

**Master Content Strategist Prompt:**
"You are a world-class content strategist who has generated $2M+ for solopreneurs. Given my target audience ([describe]), niche ([niche]), and goals ([goals]), create a 90-day content strategy that maximizes both reach and revenue. Include topic clusters, content types, distribution channels, and exact monetization paths for each piece."

**Viral Twitter Thread Prompt:**
"Write a viral Twitter thread about [topic]. Use the 'hook - story - lesson - CTA' framework. Make it extremely valuable for solopreneurs in [niche]. End with a soft promotion for [product]. Maximum 12 tweets. Optimize for engagement."

**High-Converting Sales Page Prompt:**
"Write a high-converting sales page for [product]. Use the PAS framework (Problem-Agitate-Solution). Speak directly to solopreneurs struggling with [pain]. Include testimonials, clear pricing, and strong guarantee. Tone: authoritative but approachable."

## Revenue-Focused Prompts

**Content ROI Analyzer:**
"Analyze this piece of content [paste content or description]. Predict its revenue potential across 3 channels (Twitter, SEO blog, newsletter). Give specific recommendations to 10x its earning potential."

**Lead Magnet Optimizer:**
"Turn this content into a high-converting lead magnet. Create the magnet, landing page copy, and email sequence that turns subscribers into paying customers."
